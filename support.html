About BudgetBuddy

BudgetBuddy is a comprehensive personal finance management app that helps you:

  • Track income and expenses
  • Manage multiple accounts (bank accounts, digital wallets, investments)
  • Set and monitor monthly budgets
  • Track mortgage payments and property details
  • Generate financial insights and analysis

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is my financial data secure?
A: Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device using iOS SwiftData. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your information on external servers.
Q: Can I backup my data?
A: Your BudgetBuddy data is automatically included in your device's iCloud backup if you have iCloud backup enabled in your device settings.
Q: How do I delete my data?
A: You can delete individual transactions, accounts, mortgages, or categories within the app. To delete all data, simply delete the app from your device.
Q: Why does the app need photo library access?
A: Photo library access is optional and only requested if you choose to add a profile photo. The selected photo is stored locally on your device.
Q: Does BudgetBuddy work offline?
A: Yes! Since all data is stored locally on your device, BudgetBuddy works completely offline without requiring an internet connection.
Q: Can I use BudgetBuddy on multiple devices?
A: Currently, data is stored locally on each device. If you have iCloud backup enabled, you can restore data to a new device through device restore.
Q: What currencies are supported?
A: BudgetBuddy supports all major currencies. You can set your preferred currency in the app settings.
