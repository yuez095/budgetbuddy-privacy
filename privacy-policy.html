Privacy Policy for BudgetBuddy

Last Updated: January 26, 2026

Overview

BudgetBuddy ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

BudgetBuddy stores all data locally on your device. We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on external servers.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device:

  • Financial Transactions: Income and expense records
  • Account Information: Bank accounts, digital wallets, and investment accounts you add
  • Budget Settings: Monthly budget limits and categories
  • Mortgage Information: Property details, loan amounts, interest rates, and payment schedules
  • Profile Information: Name, email (optional), and profile photo (if provided)
  • Categories: Custom spending categories you create

How We Use Your Information

All data is used solely within the app to:

  • Track your income and expenses
  • Manage your budget
  • Monitor mortgage payments
  • Generate financial insights
  • Display your account balances

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device using iOS SwiftData
  • Your profile photo is stored locally using UserDefaults
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No third-party analytics or tracking services are used
  • Your data is protected by your device's security measures (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)

Photo Library Access

If you choose to add a profile photo, the app will request access to your photo library. This permission is optional and only used to select a profile picture. The selected photo is stored locally on your device.

Data Backup

Your BudgetBuddy data may be included in your device's iCloud backup if you have iCloud backup enabled. This is controlled by your device settings, not by the app.

Children's Privacy

BudgetBuddy does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is designed for adult users managing personal finances.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data by:

  1. Deleting individual transactions, accounts, mortgages, or categories within the app
  2. Deleting the app from your device, which removes all locally stored data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You have complete control over your data
  • You can export your data through device backups
  • You can delete all data by removing the app
  • No data is shared with third parties

Third-Party Services

BudgetBuddy does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks.

Consent

By using BudgetBuddy, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
